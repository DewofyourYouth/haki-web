## Overview

Haki uses account and progress data to provide the language-learning experience, save lesson progress, and improve the app.

## Information We Use

Depending on how you use the app, Haki may use:

- Account information used to sign in.
- Lesson progress, XP, settings, and practice history.
- Device, browser, and diagnostic information needed to troubleshoot issues.
- Messages you send to support.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to run the app, sync progress, fix bugs, respond to support requests, and understand which parts of the learning experience need improvement.

## Third-Party Services

Haki may use services such as Firebase for authentication, data storage, analytics, or app diagnostics, and text-to-speech providers for audio generation.

## Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email **apps@dewofyouryouth.com**.
