We're preparing for the initial release of **Haki: Learn Levantine Arabic** on the Apple App Store! This release candidate includes the core features and content for our language learning app, designed to help users learn Levantine Arabic through engaging lessons and interactive practice.

Initial release candidate for **Haki: Learn Levantine Arabic**.

## Highlights

- Map-based progression through Levantine locations.
- Short practice sessions with XP and level progress.
- Vocabulary, phrase, grammar, and topic lessons.
- Audio playback for listening practice.
- Support and privacy pages prepared for App Store review.

## Known Issues

- Mobile App Store links are not published yet.
- Some curriculum and audio content may continue to be revised after launch.
